Welcome to Taniti

Explore the vibrant culinary scene in Taniti, where you can savor a variety of flavors from local specialties to international cuisine

Local Fish and Rice

Indulge in the traditional tastes of Taniti with our selection of five restaurants specializing in local fish and rice dishes, offering a true taste of the island's culinary heritage.

American-Style Meals

Craving a taste of home? Our three American-style restaurants serve up familiar favorites, from burgers to salads, ensuring comfort food is never far away.

Pan-Asian Cuisine

Embark on a culinary journey through Asia with our two Pan-Asian cuisine restaurants, featuring a fusion of flavors from across the continent.

Grocery Stores

For those preferring to cook their own meals or needing a quick snack, Taniti is home to two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and a 24-hour convenience store.
