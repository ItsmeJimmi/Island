Welcome to Taniti

Explore the great activities that we offer, each promising unique experiences and unforgettable memories.

Snorkeling

Immerse yourself in the crystal-clear waters of Taniti and discover the vibrant underwater world teeming with colorful marine life.

Hiking

Embark on scenic trails that lead you through lush rainforests, past cascading waterfalls, and to breathtaking viewpoints overlooking the island.

Zip-lining

Feel the thrill of zip-lining as you soar above the treetops, enjoying panoramic views of the island's stunning landscapes.

Nightlife

Enjoy visiting one of our several pubs, including a microbrewery, or dancing at our new dance club.
